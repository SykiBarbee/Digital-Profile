Academic Writing

Black Representation in the United States: Past , Present and Future (PDF)
I wrote this 19 page research paper for my Ideas That Change the World honors class. After watching Black Panther, and seeing Black Americans joyous and proud of their heritage made me want to write this paper. Everyone wants to be able to watch television, movies, music, theater and magazines with people who look like them. Black Americans have not had to luxury to see themselves represented in positive, non-stereotypical roles, with the exception of a few television shows (Fresh Prince and The Cosby Show).

The Unmasking Of King Henry V in Shakespeare's Henry V (PDF)
Spring semester of 2019 I took an Advanced Shakespeare course where we extensively read and analyzed Henry V. For my final research paper, I decided to focus on King Henry's character. I took on the debate amongst many Shakespearean critics where Henry Vis either seen as a noble Christian King with great military prowess, or a machiavellian King whose ego outweighs his duty to his kingdom. My research has led me to agree with the latter critics. However, I also acknowledge that he has potential to be the former, if only he sets his ego aside, and takes accountablity for his choices.
