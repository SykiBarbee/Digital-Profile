About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Syki. I am a 32 year-old Junior at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am currently majoring in English with specializations in Professional Writing and Technical Communication Creative Writing and the Arts Extension Services. I am also a fiction editor for the UMass Amherst Jabberwocky literary magazine.

Outside of school, I am very passionate about reading. Since I was a child, I'd read anything I could get my hands on. One of my all-time favorite childhood books is Are You There God? It's Me Margaret by Judy Blume. As an adult, my favorite series are Harry Potter and Miss Perigrines Home for Peculiar Children. However, I love all types of genres including, thriller, memoirs, fantasy, historical fiction and creative non-fiction.

Professionally, I hope to help non-profits by writing grants. I also want to own my own publishing company for marginalized groups of people. Personally, I would love to see more stories about people like me, who live intersectional lives. I am African American, bisexual, married to a woman of color, and I also have an invisible disability. Reading books for people like me, by people like me is something I find very important.
